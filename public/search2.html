What is Omegrep?

Omegrep helps you find interesting chat logs from Omegle.com.

Are you affiliated with Omegle?

Omegrep is not affiliated with Omegle in any way.

How did you get these chat logs?

All saved logs are publicly available on Omegle.com. Simply visit logs.omegle.com/#####, replacing ##### with any 5, 6 or 7 digit hexadecimal number. Some numbers will give you a 403 error, but most will not. Here is an example.

Why isn't my chat log showing up in search?

Theres a lot of logs out there, so unfortunately it may take us some time to get to yours. We have plans to add the ability to "submit" a chat log. Send us an email to hurry us up if you'd be interested in that.

Why is the text in search results wrong sometimes?

Omegle saves logs as images, so we have to get the computer to "read" the images into text files. Reading screenshots is hard for computers, and sometimes he makes mistakes. Please forgive him!

Why did you create this?

For practice and entertainment.

Did you "hack" Omegle?

We did not hack Omegle. All the information used on this website is publicly available on Omegle.com. We simply organized it. See Question 3 for more details.

How do I get a chat log removed?

If you find a chat log containing your personal information, or links to to something illegal or horrific, let us know via email or the report button and we will remove it ASAP. Unfortunately, removing it from here does not remove it from Omegle's servers, so we'd recommend contacting Omegle, and generally not giving your personal information to strangers on the internet.

What does Omegrep's name mean?

Omegrep is a combination of "Omegle" and "grep", a command line utility used for searching through text.

How can I contact you?

Email us about anything at all at hello@omegrep.com.
